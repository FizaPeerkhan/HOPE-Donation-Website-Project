Select a city to explore NGOs working for education in your area. Learn about their causes, initiatives, and how you can support them.

Teach For India

Founded in 2009, Teach For India recruits college graduates and professionals to serve as full-time teachers in low-income schools for two years, aiming to eliminate educational inequity.

BHUMI

Established in 2006, BHUMI is one of India's largest independent youth volunteer non-profit organizations, focusing on education, civic engagement, environment, and social entrepreneurship across multiple cities, including Mumbai.

Toybank

Founded in August 2004, Toybank promotes 'Development Through Play' by providing toys and setting up play centers for underprivileged children, fostering physical, mental, and behavioral development.

Pratham

Established in 1995, Pratham works towards providing quality education to underprivileged children in Mumbai and other parts of India, aiming to improve literacy and learning outcomes.

Yuva Unstoppable

Founded in 2005, Yuva Unstoppable focuses on providing underprivileged children with access to education, sanitation, drinking water, and various non-curricular activities across multiple cities, including Mumbai.

Apnalaya

Established in 1973, Apnalaya focuses on empowering marginalized communities in Mumbai, with programs addressing education, child welfare, and health.
